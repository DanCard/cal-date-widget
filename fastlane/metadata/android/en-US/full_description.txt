Cal Date Widget is a small, early-release app that adds simple date and calendar widgets to your home screen. It works, but it is still young — expect some rough edges, and please report anything that looks off.

THE THREE WIDGETS

1. Date widget
Shows the current date. Pick a preset format or type your own pattern (e.g. EEEE, d MMM).

2. Daily calendar widget
Shows today's events from your device calendars, and adds more day columns automatically if you make the widget wider. Once today's events are over, it moves on to tomorrow.

3. Weekly calendar widget
A 7-day view of upcoming events. Today's column is wider than the others, and text size adjusts to fit the available space.

CUSTOMIZATION
Text color, shadows, and background transparency are adjustable per widget. Tap a widget to open your calendar app or its settings.

PRIVACY
The app has no internet permission, so your calendar data cannot leave your device. No ads, no trackers, no analytics. The source code is publicly viewable on GitHub.

KNOWN LIMITATIONS
• Developed and tested mainly on one device (a Samsung Galaxy Z Fold4). Other devices and launchers may show layout quirks.
• Widgets refresh periodically and when tapped, not instantly after every calendar change.
• The interface is English-only for now.

If something breaks or looks wrong, use the in-app bug report — it prepares a diagnostic email you can review before sending. Every report is read.
