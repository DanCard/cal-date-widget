Transform your home screen with a set of sleek, custom widgets designed to keep you organized and respect your privacy. Cal Date Widget offers three highly-customizable widgets to fit your unique style.

THE THREE WIDGETS

1. Date Widget: A self-resizing date display. Text auto-scales perfectly to fit any size, down to 1x1. Choose from beautiful preset formats or define your own SimpleDateFormat pattern.

2. Daily Calendar Widget: Shows today's upcoming events. It automatically advances to show tomorrow's agenda once the current day's events are done.

3. Weekly Calendar Widget: A smart 7-day overview. Today's column is wider to emphasize what's next. Past days automatically shift forward to show next week's corresponding days so you never see blank, stale columns.

PREMIUM CUSTOMIZATION
• Beautiful Color Palettes: Customize text, backgrounds, and shadows with a precision RGB color picker.
• Dynamic Font Scaling: Per-column binary search text scaling ensures your event titles auto-fit perfectly without clipping.
• Opacity Control: Adjust background transparency to blend seamlessly with any wallpaper.
• Quick Navigation: Tap any widget to instantly open your default system calendar or configuration settings.
• Multi-Level Undo: Experiment with designs risk-free with unlimited undo/redo on configuration screens.

100% PRIVACY-FIRST
• No Internet Permission: The app is completely offline. Your calendar data never leaves your device.
• Open Source: Auditable source code available on GitHub.
• No Ads, No Trackers, No Analytics: We do not collect, share, or monetize any of your data.
