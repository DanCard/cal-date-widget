Cal Date Widget brings sleek, premium, and fully customizable calendar widgets directly to your Android home screen. Stay organized at a glance with a minimalist design that fits beautifully with any wallpaper or theme.

The app includes three distinct, powerful widgets:

1. DATE WIDGET (1x1 & larger)
• A clean, minimal widget displaying the current date.
• Choose from multiple pre-set formats or define your own custom pattern (e.g., EEEE, d MMM).

2. DAILY CALENDAR WIDGET
• A dynamic daily planner widget that adjusts the number of columns (1 to 7 days) automatically based on the widget’s width on your screen.
• Smart Start Day: Automatically shifts to show the next day's schedule once all of today's events have elapsed, keeping you focused on what's next.

3. WEEKLY CALENDAR WIDGET
• A comprehensive 7-day view of your calendar events.
• Today Highlight: Today’s column is weighted larger, making it easy to focus on today's agenda.
• Dynamic Font Scaling: Uses an advanced per-column sizing algorithm to automatically scale text to perfectly fit the widget space.
• Near-Duplicate Filtering & Rotation: Intelligently groups matching events (such as multiple shifts or duplicates) and rotates through them minute-by-minute so your screen stays clean without missing details.
• Past Event Dimming: Events that have already passed are displayed in a smaller, subtler font to keep your layout organized.

PREMIUM CUSTOMIZATION
• Complete control over text color, shadow color, and background transparency (70% dark mode transparency included by default for optimal contrast).
• Increased text shadow radius and offset for maximum readability over any background.
• Tap widgets to open settings or launch your calendar app instantly.

100% PRIVACY FIRST
• Works entirely offline.
• No internet permission requested – your calendar data never leaves your device.
• Source-available and free of trackers, ads, or third-party analytics.
