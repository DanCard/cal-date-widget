# Privacy Policy for Cal Date Widget

**Effective Date:** June 9, 2026

Daniel Cardenas ("we," "us," or "our") operates the **Cal Date Widget** mobile application (the "Service"). This page informs you of our policies regarding the collection, use, and disclosure of personal data when you use our Service and the choices you have associated with that data.

## 1. Information Collection and Use

We do **not** collect, store, or transmit any personal information through the Service. The application is designed to function entirely on your device without the need for an account or internet connection for its core features.

## 2. Permissions and Data Usage

To provide the widget functionality, the Service requests specific permissions on your Android device:

### Calendar Access (`READ_CALENDAR`)
*   **Purpose:** The app requires access to your device's calendar to retrieve event titles, times, and dates.
*   **Usage:** This data is used exclusively to render the calendar events directly onto the home screen widgets you have configured.
*   **Storage:** Calendar data is processed in real-time or cached temporarily in the app's local memory for rendering purposes. It is **never** uploaded to any server, shared with third parties, or stored permanently outside of your device's own calendar system.

## 3. Data Safety and Security

Since we do not collect any personal data, we do not have any "data" to secure or share. Your calendar information remains under your control via the Android operating system's security model.

## 4. Third-Party Services

The Service does not use third-party analytics, advertising, or tracking services.

## 5. Children's Privacy

Our Service does not address anyone under the age of 13. We do not knowingly collect personally identifiable information from children under 13.

## 6. Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may update our Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify you of any changes by posting the new Privacy Policy on this page. You are advised to review this Privacy Policy periodically for any changes.
